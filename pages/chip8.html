Description

Since I was a child I have played with emulators. I knew they are complex to programe. But I understimated how complex this task can be.

People experienced building emulator recommend to start digging in this world by doing the chip8. Hence this is the kind of proyect we could consider the "Hello World" of emulation.

Personally I enjoyed this project like a child. It was very funny to come back and review some low-level computing fundamentals.


I would like to recommend everyone to give a try to chip8 emulation and do your own implementation.
